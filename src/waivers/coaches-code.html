JOHNSTON COUNTY ATHLETIC ASSOCIATION

COACHES CODE OF CONDUCT

I hereby pledge to provide positive support, care, and encouragement for all children participating in youth sports by following this Coaches' Code of Conduct Pledge:

  • I will encourage good sportsmanship by demonstrating positive support for all players, opposing coaches, and officials at every game, practice, or other youth sports event.
  • I will place the emotional and physical well-being of my players ahead of a personal desire to win.
  • I will insist that my players play in a safe and healthy environment.
  • I will support opposing coaches and officials working with my team, in order to encourage a positive and enjoyable experience for all.
  • I will demand a sports environment for our players that is free of drugs, tobacco, and alcohol and will refrain from their use at all youth sports events.
  • I will remember that the game is for youth - not coaches.
  • I will give positive reinforcement to all players.
  • I will coach ALL of my players evenly and fairly.
  • I will give equal playing time to ALL players on my team.
  • I will do my very best to make youth sports fun for all players on my team.
  • I will ask my players to treat other players, coaches, fans, and officials with respect regardless of race, sex, creed, or ability.
  • I will encourage my players at all times and teach them that honest effort is as important (maybe even more important) than winning.
  • I will respect and understand parents concerns and questions, both on and off the court.
  • I will accept that officials are doing the best job they can and not be critical.
  • I will keep my emotions under control.
  • I will remember that my players will get the most out of sports with my love and positive support.

JOHNSTON COUNTY ATHLETIC ASSOCIATION

PARENTS CODE OF CONDUCT

I hereby pledge to provide positive support, care, and encouragement for my child participating in youth sports by following this Parents' Code of Ethics Pledge:

  • I will encourage good sportsmanship by demonstrating positive support for all players, coaches, and officials at every game, practice, or other youth sports event.
  • I will place the emotional and physical well-being of my child ahead of a personal desire to win.
  • I will remember that children participate in our leagues to have fun and that the game is for children, NOT adults.
  • I will support coaches and officials working with my child, in order to encourage a positive and enjoyable experience for all.
  • I will demand a sports environment for my child that is free of drugs, tobacco, and alcohol and will refrain from their use at all youth sports events.
  • I (and my guests) will follow the rules and regulations of the Smithfield Parks & Recreation Department.
  • I (and my guests) will be a positive role model for my child and encourage sportsmanship by showing respect and courtesy at all times.
  • I will not engage in any form of harassment, taunting, booing, or use of profane language or gestures towards coaches, players, fans, or officials before, during, or at any time after a sporting event.
  • I will require that my child's coach be trained in the responsibilities of being a youth sports coach and that the coach upholds the Coaches Code of Ethics.
  • I will not post any derogatory remarks or commentary about the league, coaches, parents, or players on any social media and I will not allow my child to do so.
  • I will not encourage any behaviors or practices that could endanger the health and well-being of players.
  • I will respect and support my child's coach and let him/her coach during the game. If I have a concern, I will not discuss it at the game field or in front of players or other parents, but will take the time to discuss it at an agreed upon time and location with a league representative present if necessary.
  • I will keep my emotions under control.
  • I will teach my child to treat other players, coaches, umpires and officials, and spectators with respect regardless of race, creed, color, sex, or ability.
  • I will remember that my child will get the most out of sports with my love and positive support.
